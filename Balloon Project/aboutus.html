About Us

"Reflections Of Africa"

Miracle Experience is an innovative game-changer in the tourism industry in Tanzania. The company, which operates as a hot air balloon safari company, is managed by professionals with vast experience in the industry.
Our base camps are in prime areas of the Serengeti offering the most breathtaking views of the savannah while tracking the famous migration of the wildebeest. We have seasonal bases in Kirawira (June to October), Kogatende (June to November), Ndutu (December
to March) and a permanent base in Seronera (Center of Serengeti), operating all year round. We guarantee you an exclusive experience from heights of the latest and state-of-the art Ultra Magic hot air balloons. During the flight, you will enjoy an uninterrupted bird's-eye view of “Siringet” the place of endless plains as referred to by the inhabiting Maasai
.We hope you will soar the Serengeti skies with us and experience the Miracle you will recall for generations to come!
